Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty on a bun or roll. Hamburgers are traditionally made with ground beef and served with onions, tomatoes, lettuce, ketchup, and other garnishes.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Patty

Steps

  1. Prepare
  2. Preheat a grill
  3. Make the patties, pat them into a disk
  4. Cook the patties on the prepared grill
  5. Join all the ingredients together like a sandwich
  6. Eat it!
