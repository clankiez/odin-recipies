Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food made from a mixture of flour, eggs, and water that is formed into different shapes and then boiled. Spaghetti, macaroni, and noodles are types of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Large pot
  • Boiled water
  • Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare
  2. Load the large pot with water
  3. Salt the water
  4. Bring the water to a boiling status
  5. Stir the pasta to keep it from sticking
  6. Drain the water once it's ready
  7. Serve in a plate with the sauce
  8. Eat it!
